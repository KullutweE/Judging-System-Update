How it works

In this section we will work you through how you can apply for the upcoming Mr and Miss IUM beauty pageant. Follow the easy steps in order for you to successfully apply. Or if you have been nominated to judge in this pageant we have also provided easy steps on how to start your judging journey.
To apply please select apply in the navigation bar to direct you to the application page. In order to apply you must meet the requirements, if you are unable to apply it means that you do not meet the minimum requirements to participate in the competition.

After you click apply fill in the information needed and if you meet the requirements you will be directed to the application form. Fill in all the information in the application form and click submitt, if you would like to start over with your application just click refresh and you may fill in and submit. After subitting successfully you will see a notification telling you it was a successful submission, if you followed all instructions and it gives you an error when submitting, try again or contact the facilitator.

If you are a chosen judge and would like to login click on signin in the navigation bar, this will take you to a login button, click it and fill in your username and password received via email or put the new password if you reset the previouse one. Once you login you will be able to access your Judge portal. Or Log in as Admin to access admin portal.
